Our Story

Where Good Coffee
Meets Great Company

Robusta Cafe was born with one simple belief — that a great cup of coffee can change the tone of your whole day. Located in the heart of Hinjawadi's tech hub, we're the go-to spot for developers, dreamers, and everyone in between.

From rich espressos to velvety shakes and freshly prepared Tawa Pulao, our menu is crafted with quality and soul — all at prices that make every day feel like a treat.
